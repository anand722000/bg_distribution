Purpose

Gita Jayanti, celebrated globally by ISKCON, commemorates the divine moment when Lord Krishna revealed the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna. To honor this sacred day, devotees distribute the Gita widely, sharing its eternal wisdom to inspire spiritual growth and devotion.

Benefit

As stated in Bhagavad Gita 18.68–69, those who share this supreme knowledge are most dear to Lord Krishna and are assured of pure devotional service and ultimate return to Him.

Gita Daan is Maha Daan

Donating the Bhagavad Gita brings immense benefits to both the giver and the receiver. It spreads the knowledge of the absolute truth, offering individuals a chance to perfect their lives. There are no downsides—only spiritual upliftment and positive transformation.

The Bhagavad Gita teaches the right way to live. By donating copies, we help spread this spiritual wisdom, offering people meaningful solutions to their material challenges and guiding them toward a purposeful life.

Distribution service

Serve Lord Krishna by distributing His divine message. Each Gita you share brings someone closer to Him and earns His eternal blessings.

Spread knowledge

Empower the next generation—give them the wisdom of the Gita to face life’s challenges with clarity, courage, and conviction. Your contribution can uplift entire communities with timeless knowledge.
